Definition of Terms

A VARIABLE is a symbol that may take different values.
It is usually represented by letters like x, y, a, b, m, n, and p.

A VARIABLE can be DEPENDENT or INDEPENDENT.

See the examples below:

Independent Variables

Independent Variable is a variable that stands ALONE and whose value does NOT depend on other variable.
It may also be referred as the INPUT of the function.

Dependent Variable

Dependent Variable is something that DEPENDS on another factors and is AFFECTED by the changes on the independent variable.
It may also be referred as the OUTPUT of the function.

Examples of Dependent and Independent Variables

Independent Variables Dependent Variables
number of tokens you have number of minutes you can play in arcade games
number of kilowatts consumed in a month amount of monthly electric bill
number of GB in your monthly subscription number of hours you can surf the web
